Universal Exports

Taiwan & China • Est. 1995

The Engine of Global Sourcing

Founded in 1995, Universal Exports is our flagship company and the headquarters for the Universal Group. With over 30 years of industry experience, this division excels in manufacturing, wholesale buying, quality control, and logistics.

Our teams in Taiwan, Guangzhou, and Yiwu work with a vast network of factories and suppliers to serve clients on every continent, from national chain stores to large wholesalers.

Universal Belize

Belize • Est. 2002

Gateway to Central America

Comprising Unitex and Universal Freezone, our Belize operations are a cornerstone of our presence in the Americas. Founded in 2002, this division has over 20 years of deep market experience.

We operate as a leading wholesale distributor and retailer, serving local Belizean cities and the neighboring Mexican market through the Corozal Freezone. This strategic location allows us to efficiently manage distribution for our private labels and exclusive brand partners throughout the region.

Univex

USA • Est. 2019

Driving Strategy and Innovation

Established in 2019 in New York City, Univex solidifies our presence in the dominant US market. This division is responsible for the broad strategic initiatives that propel the entire Universal Group forward.

Key functions include establishing authorized distributor partnerships with major international brands, developing our e-commerce strategy, and spearheading technology adoption to modernize our global operations.

Expanding to Mexico in 2025

We are excited to announce our upcoming expansion into Latin America.

Our new operations will provide comprehensive, cross-border trade solutions, further strengthening our global network and better serving our clients in the Americas. Stay tuned for more details.
