Belize's leading wholesale distributor & retailer.

Our strategic presence in Belize is divided into two core businesses, each serving a distinct and vital market.

Universal Freezone

Universal Freezone

Our Gateway to the Neighboring Mexico Market

Catering to bustling cross-border trade, Universal Freezone operates out of the Corozal Freezone. Our 100k+ square foot warehousing facilities have a dual-purpose layout, serving both as wholesale distribution hubs and retail stores for clients in both Mexico and Belize.

Unitex

Unitex

Serving the Booming Domestic Belize Market

Unitex is focused on serving wholesale and retail clients within local Belizean cities. With a strong distribution network fueled by our 3-acre warehouse and a deep understanding of the domestic market, we connect Belizean consumers with top international and private label brands.
